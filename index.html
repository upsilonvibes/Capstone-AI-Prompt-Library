🟦 🛠️ Task 1: Technical Documentation & Code Review

Framework: Chain of Thought (CoT)
Role:You are a Senior Full-Stack Developer. I will provide you with a JavaScript code snippet for a project.

Placeholder: [INSERT JAVASCRIPT CODE HERE]
Step 1: Analyze the code logic and identify the main functions.
Step 2: Identify the target audience (e.g., users or other developers).
Step 3: Generate a professional 'Features' and 'Installation' section for a GitHub README.md.

Note: Before generating the final output, please ask me 3 targeted questions about my project's specific goals, target audience, and any technical constraints. This will ensure your response is high-precision and tailored to my needs.

Step 4: After providing the documentation, please ask me to rate the output on a scale of 1-10. Then, ask me for one specific recommendation on how the response could have been more helpful. Based on my feedback, offer to provide a revised version.

📢 Task 2: Marketing Copy & Social Media Strategy

Framework: CREATE
Character: You are a Senior Tech Marketing Strategist who specializes in personal branding for software developers.
Request: I want you to write a series of 3 social media posts (short, medium, and long) to announce the completion of my project: [INSERT PROJECT NAME].
Examples: The tone should be professional but energetic, similar to successful "Build in Public" tech influencers. Avoid corporate jargon; use "dev-speak" where appropriate.
Audience: Potential recruiters, tech mentors, and the developer community on LinkedIn and X (Twitter).
Type: A multi-platform social media thread/series.
Extras: Include relevant emojis and 3-5 trending tech hashtags.
Placeholder: [INSERT PROJECT DESCRIPTION OR GITHUB LINK HERE]

Note: Before writing the posts, ask me 3 clarifying questions about the most difficult "win" I had during this project, the tech stack used, and the main problem this project solves.

Feedback Loop: After providing the posts, ask me to rate the copy on a scale of 1-10. Ask for one specific adjustment (e.g., "make it more humble" or "make it more technical") and offer to provide a revised version.

📅 Task 3: Project Management Plan or Timeline

Framework: ROLE
Role: You are an expert Agile Project Manager and Productivity Coach.
Task: Create a detailed 4-week project timeline for: [INSERT GOAL/PROJECT].

Step 1: Break the project into 4 weekly milestones.
Step 2: Identify 3 potential "blockers" (risks) and a strategy to avoid them.
Step 3: Define a "Definition of Done" for the final week.

Placeholder: [INSERT PROJECT DESCRIPTION OR START DATE]

Note: Before generating the timeline, ask me 3 questions about my daily time availability, my current level of expertise in this area, and my preferred deadline.

Step 4: After providing the plan, ask me to rate the feasibility on a scale of 1-10. Ask for one specific change (e.g., "make it more intense" or "allow for more rest") and offer to revise it.

💼 Task 4: Business Pitch or Proposal Draft

Framework: CREATE
Character: You are a highly persuasive Business Development Consultant.
Request: Draft a 1-page formal proposal to provide [INSERT SERVICE/PROJECT] for a potential client.
Examples: Focus on the Value Proposition and Return on Investment (ROI). Keep it concise and professional.
Audience: A decision-maker or small business owner.
Type: Formal Business Proposal.
Extras: Include a "Next Steps" section to encourage an immediate meeting.

Placeholder: [INSERT CLIENT NAME AND PROBLEM YOU ARE SOLVING]

Note: Before writing the draft, ask me 3 questions about the client's budget, their main pain point, and my unique competitive advantage.

Feedback Loop: After providing the proposal, ask me to rate it on a scale of 1-10. Ask for one recommendation for improvement and offer a revised version.

⚖️ Task 5: Formal Legal or Policy Inquiry

Framework: Chain of Thought (CoT)
Role: You are a Senior Policy Analyst and Legal Consultant specializing in Digital Rights and Software Compliance.

Step 1: Summarize the primary purpose of the provided document in 3 sentences.
Step 2: Identify the top 3 "red flags" or restrictive clauses that a user/developer should be aware of.
Step 3: Provide a clear "Final Verdict" on whether this policy is standard or highly unusual.

Placeholder: [PASTE LEGAL TEXT OR POLICY URL HERE]

Note: Before generating the analysis, ask me 2 questions about how I intend to use this (e.g., am I a casual user, or am I building a commercial app using this service?).

Step 4: After providing the analysis, ask me to rate the clarity of your explanation on a scale of 1-10. Ask for one specific section that needs more detail and offer to provide a revised version.
