## 🛠 Task 1: Technical Documentation & Code Review
**Framework:** Chain of Thought (CoT)  
**Role:** Senior Full-Stack Developer  

### The Prompt
> "You are a Senior Full-Stack Developer. I will provide you with a JavaScript code snippet for a project.
Step 1: Analyze the code logic and identify the main functions.
Step 2: Identify the target audience (e.g., users or other developers).
Step 3: Generate a professional 'Features' and 'Installation' section for a GitHub README.md.
Note: Before generating the final output, please ask me 3 targeted questions about my project's specific goals, target audience, and any technical constraints.
Step 4: After providing the documentation, please ask me to rate the output on a scale of 1-10 and offer to provide a revised version."

### 🧪 Example Output [Tested on: Password-Checker (percysmartanecdote168-droid/Password-Checker)]
* **AI Feedback:** "Before I proceed, I have 3 questions regarding your project's specific goals..."
* **Quality Score:** 9/10
* **Justification:** The precision prompting technique ensured the output was tailored to the specific security features of the script.

## 📢 Task 2: Marketing Copy & Social Strategy
**Framework:** CREATE  
**Character:** Senior Tech Marketing Strategist  

### The Prompt
> "Character: You are a Senior Tech Marketing Strategist who specializes in personal branding for software developers.
Request: I want you to write a series of 3 social media posts (short, medium, and long) to announce the completion of my project.
Examples: The tone should be professional but energetic, similar to successful 'Build in Public' tech influencers.
Audience: Potential recruiters and the developer community.
Extras: Include relevant emojis and 3-5 trending tech hashtags.
Note: Before writing the posts, ask me 3 clarifying questions about the most difficult 'win' I had, the tech stack, and the main problem solved."

### 🧪 Example Output
* **Quality Score:** 10/10
* **Justification:** Successfully generated three distinct tones for different social platforms.

## 📅 Task 3: Project Management Plan
**Framework:** ROLE  
**Role:** Agile Project Manager  

### The Prompt
> "Role: You are an expert Agile Project Manager and Productivity Coach. Create a detailed 4-week project timeline for: [INSERT GOAL/PROJECT].

Step 1: Break the project into 4 weekly milestones with specific deliverables.
Step 2: Identify 3 potential 'blockers' (risks) based on my expertise level and provide a strategy to avoid them.
Step 3: Define a clear 'Definition of Done' for the final week.

Note: Before generating the timeline, please ask me 3 questions about my daily time availability, my current level of expertise in this area, and my preferred final deadline.

Step 4: After providing the plan, ask me to rate the feasibility on a scale of 1-10 and offer to adjust the intensity based on my feedback."

## 💼 Task 4: Business Pitch or Proposal
**Framework:** CREATE  
**Character:** Business Development Consultant  

### The Prompt
> "Character: You are a highly persuasive Business Development Consultant.
Request: Draft a 1-page formal proposal to provide [INSERT SERVICE/PROJECT] for a potential client.
Examples: Focus heavily on the Value Proposition and the Return on Investment (ROI) for the client. Keep the language concise and professional.
Audience: A key decision-maker or small business owner.
Type: Formal Business Proposal.
Extras: Include a 'Next Steps' section that encourages an immediate follow-up meeting.

Note: Before writing the draft, ask me 3 questions about the client's estimated budget, their main business pain point, and my unique competitive advantage.

Feedback loop: After providing the proposal, ask me to rate the persuasiveness on a scale of 1-10 and offer a revised version."

## ⚖️ Task 5: Legal or Policy Inquiry
**Framework:** Chain of Thought (CoT)  
**Role:** Senior Policy Analyst  

### The Prompt
> "You are a Senior Policy Analyst and Legal Consultant specializing in Digital Rights and Software Compliance. I will provide you with a policy or legal text: [PASTE TEXT OR URL].

Step 1: Summarize the primary purpose and scope of the provided document in exactly 3 sentences.
Step 2: Identify the top 3 'red flags' or restrictive clauses that a user or developer should be aware of regarding data usage or liability.
Step 3: Provide a clear 'Final Verdict' on whether this policy is industry-standard or highly unusual/risky.

Note: Before generating the analysis, ask me 2 questions about my specific context (e.g., am I a casual user, or am I building a commercial app using this service?).

Step 4: After providing the analysis, ask me to rate the clarity of the explanation on a scale of 1-10 and offer to explain a specific clause in more detail."
